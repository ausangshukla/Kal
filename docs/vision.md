# Vision — why Jor exists

## The goal

Make Kal **10x** — across all of life, not one metric. The first three domains, chosen
because they compound into everything else:

1. **Mind** — mental health, calm, focus. Jor as psychologist/monk and meditation guide.
2. **Money** — financial fitness. Jor as an advisor who *forces* a monthly review-and-improve
   ritual (accountability, not just advice).
3. **Body** — physical practice. Jor as a yoga teacher who can *see* Kal's poses and
   correct them (10 common poses in V1).

## Who is Kal

**V1 is built for one person: the founder.** No personas, no market research. If Jor
doesn't change the founder's life daily, nothing else matters. Generalizing to other
Kals is a post-V1 concern; the open-source community is how it happens.

## Coach, teacher, or companion?

All three — but **the active skill decides the mix**, one persona at a time:

- The Monk is mostly *companion* (warmth, listening) with teacher moments (meditation).
- The Financial Advisor is mostly *coach* (authority, permission to nag, ritual enforcement).
- The Yoga Teacher is *teacher* (demonstrate, observe, correct).

Who Jor "is" right now is made unambiguous through the canvas (e.g., a title/theme) and
subtle cues in the face/voice. A **global override** lets Kal pin a persona style across
all skills if desired.

## Measuring 10x

**Deferred to V2.** V1 succeeds on a softer bar: Kal voluntarily returns to Jor every day,
completes the monthly finance ritual, and practices yoga with corrections that actually
improve form. Quantified tracking (metrics dashboards, streaks, progress graphs) is a V2
feature — see [roadmap.md](roadmap.md).

## Non-goals for V1

- Physical hardware (simulator only — see [simulator.md](simulator.md))
- Movement / degrees of freedom
- Multiple simultaneous personas
- Skill marketplace
- Quantified self-tracking
